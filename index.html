Quick Setup

  1. Add a MaintenanceLog tab to your Google Sheet
  2. Add columns: Property, Issue, Guest Message, Guest Name, Priority (urgent/high/medium/low), Status (open/in-progress/resolved/completed), Reported Date, Resolved Date, Notes
  3. In your Google Sheet, go to Extensions → Apps Script, paste the webhook receiver code, and deploy as a Web App
  4. Copy the Apps Script URL into HostBuddy's webhook settings
  5. Paste your Sheet ID in Settings (gear icon)
